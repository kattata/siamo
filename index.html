Siamo Street

Siamo Street

Siamo Street is a young event organiser start-up based on a social media platform which is led by 3 students from Business Academy Aarhus who are crazy passionate about football.

Values

Values

In Siamo Street we have 3 main objectives:

Street football is for everyone

To grow a friendly community where everyone is welcome anytime no matter their football interest, skills or knowledge about football.

Different formats

To provide our community with stable street style football events of 4 different formats.

Social Media driven

To do live and online promotion for sport and youth oriented brands through our social events.
